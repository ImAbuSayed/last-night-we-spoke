[Hook]
When the moon hangs low and the fire burns down
I can still feel your breath on my skin
It’s a long dirt road to the edge of town
But tonight I’m letting you in

[Verse 1]
We sat on the porch while the rain fell soft
And the shadows danced on the floor
Every word you whispered took my heart aloft
Leaving me aching for more

[Pre-Chorus]
Now the clock strikes twelve and the world goes blind
And my hands reach out in the dark
There’s a fever growing in the back of my mind
Just waiting for you to spark

[Chorus]
Last night we spoke without making a sound
With the pull of a rising tide
You’re the sweetest secret that I ever found
With nowhere left to hide

[Post-Chorus]
Nowhere left to hide, falling deep and slow
You’re the only place I wanna go
